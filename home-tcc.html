You will be practice-ready the day you receive your diploma, with the ability to work in any health care setting, serve any patient population, and get involved in chiropractic research and/ or teaching. Experience all aspects of practice during Trimester 7 and Trimester 8 as you intern at Moody Health Center, practicing under a licensed chiropractor.

Academic Program

Your time at TCC will consist of 10 trimesters of evidence-based curriculum. Students become a “Master of One” through focusing on the most common technique – the Diversified technique.

With small classes and attentive professors, TCC provides a far more personalized education than most chiropractic schools. Our professors have extensive real-world experience as clinical practitioners, researchers, and scientists, enabling them to support you academically and facilitate your transition into your chiropractic career.
